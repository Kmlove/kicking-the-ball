Welcome to Kicking the Ball!

Objective:
To score goals!! "Kick" the ball around the field, avoding the defenders and dribbling the ball out of bounds and score as many goals as possible in 60 seconds.

Instructions:
Use the arrows on your keypad to move the ball forward, back, left, and right. When you're ready to start a round, press the green START button. This will allow you to move the ball around the field as well as start the countdown. Once the timer runs out, you can press the red RESET button to reset the timer and ball. You can also hit the reset button at any time during the round if you want to start over.

Have fun and good luck!

Timer:

60
